About Me

I am a researcher and engineer specializing in AI, robotics, and autonomous systems. I hold a PhD in Computer Science from Monash University, Australia, and have conducted research at the University of Regina, Canada. My work spans UAV control, multi-agent reinforcement learning, and intelligent system design.

I have led interdisciplinary projects, published in top-tier journals and conferences, and supervised graduate students. I am currently open to roles in both academia and industry, focusing on real-world intelligent systems.
