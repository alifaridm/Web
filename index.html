About Me

I am a robotics and AI researcher with over 15 years of experience bridging cutting-edge research and real-world applications. My work focuses on autonomous UAV swarms, multi-agent reinforcement learning, and embedded systems, with a mission to transform advanced robotics into practical solutions for smart agriculture, environmental monitoring, and industrial automation.

Throughout my career, I have led interdisciplinary teams, supervised graduate students, and contributed to over 20 publications in top-tier journals and conferences. I thrive on solving complex problems, from designing autonomous swarm algorithms to developing hardware-software integrated systems that operate reliably in real-world environments.

Beyond research, I am passionate about mentoring the next generation of engineers and collaborating with industry partners to translate innovations from the lab into impactful solutions. My vision is to advance robotics that are intelligent, scalable, and beneficial to society.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring new technologies, mentoring young engineers, and applying robotics principles in creative, hands-on projects.
