About Me

I am a senior robotics researcher with more than 10 years of academic and industrial experience in autonomous UAV systems, swarm robotics, multi-agent systems, and reinforcement learning.

My research focuses on developing scalable and adaptive intelligence for heterogeneous robotic systems operating in dynamic real-world environments. I have worked on UAV exploration, coordinated multi-robot spraying, multi-agent reinforcement learning, intelligent control systems, and autonomous robotic decision making.

My long-term research vision is to bridge reinforcement learning and real-world robotic deployment for applications in precision agriculture, infrastructure monitoring, search and rescue, and cooperative autonomous systems.

Current Research Directions

  • Multi-UAV Cooperative Systems
    Reinforcement learning approaches for coordinated exploration, mapping, spraying, and payload transportation using heterogeneous UAV swarms.
  • Swarm Robotics & Multi-Agent Intelligence
    Adaptive search strategies, decentralized coordination, and collective intelligence for autonomous robotic systems.
  • Intelligent Control Systems
    Development of reinforcement learning and neuro-fuzzy controllers for UAV guidance, navigation, and stabilization.
  • Autonomous Robotics for Real-World Applications
    Research on precision agriculture, infrastructure inspection, medical robotics concepts, and smart autonomous environments.

Previous Projects

  • Multi-UAV Weed Spraying System
    Developed coordinated aerial spraying strategies using reinforcement learning and multi-agent cooperation for precision agriculture applications.
  • Autonomous UAV Exploration & Mapping
    Designed reinforcement learning frameworks for collaborative exploration, coverage optimization, and autonomous mapping missions using multiple UAVs.
  • Swarm vs Swarm UAV Patrolling
    Investigated adaptive search and tracking strategies in adversarial swarm scenarios, including heterogeneous intruder behaviors and decentralized coordination.
  • Neuro-Fuzzy UAV Flight Control
    Developed ANFIS-based intelligent controllers and adaptive PID tuning methods for UAV stabilization and navigation.
  • Smart Agriculture Robotics
    Designed intelligent robotic systems for precision farming, autonomous spraying, and cooperative aerial/ground vehicle operations.
  • Medical Device R&D
    Contributed to the design and development of embedded medical systems, including PCB design, firmware development, and compliance documentation.
  • Smart Home Energy Optimization
    Applied evolutionary optimization and intelligent control methods for reducing energy and water consumption in smart environments.
  • Multi-UAV Payload Transportation
    Current research on cooperative payload lifting and control using multiple autonomous UAVs.
