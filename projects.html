Highlighted Projects

  • Energy Efficient House (1 & 2)

    Smart optimization of water temperature and flow rate to reduce energy and water waste. Neural network–based control for heating/cooling systems implemented with STM32F407 ARM core.

  • Quadcopter Research Platform

    Developed UAV testbeds with Pixhawk autopilot, Odroid XU3, XBee communication, and HD video streaming for cooperative flight experiments.

  • Swarm vs. Swarm Adaptive Search and Tracking

    Designed heterogeneous UAV/UGV swarms for scalable sense-track-net systems to locate and track rogue UAVs.

  • DC Motor Neuro-Fuzzy Controller

    Implemented ANFIS-based control with RBF and MLP identification for cost-effective DC motor control using ATmega32.

  • Online ANFIS Controller with RBF & PSO

    Developed an adaptive controller with online training for nonlinear systems, ensuring flexible and robust performance.

  • Inflight UAV Battery Interchange

    Proposed and prototyped a mid-air UAV battery replacement system to enable uninterrupted missions without return-to-base downtime.

  • Ventilator

    Emergency ventilator design for rapid deployment and field usability in medical emergencies.

  • Wound Care System (NPEWT)

    Portable vacuum-assisted wound healing device enabling at-home treatment for burns, ulcers, and surgical wounds.

  • Automated External Defibrillator (AED)

    Portable device for diagnosing and treating cardiac arrhythmias with minimal user training.

  • Drone-Delivered AEDs

    Developed UAV-based delivery of defibrillators to cardiac arrest victims, reducing response time dramatically.

  • Raspberry Pi Remote Video

    UGV controller using Raspberry Pi for wireless control and real-time video streaming to Linux-based systems.

  • RF Fractional Needling Controller

    Modernized fractional RF controller hardware using STM32 microcontroller with new features and improved reliability.

  • Multi-robot Spray System

    Cooperative UAV mapping and spraying strategies optimized with reinforcement learning and multi-objective algorithms.
