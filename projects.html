Sample Projects

Energy Efficient House (1)

Optimizes water temperature and flow rate at outlets to minimize energy and water waste, using intelligent control to maintain comfort for multiple dynamic users.

Energy Efficient House (2)

Neural-network–based control system for heating and cooling to reduce wasted energy in residential and commercial buildings. Implemented on STM32F407 ARM microcontroller.

Quadcopter Research Platform

Developed a quadrotor platform using Pixhawk, Odroid XU3, and XBee links. Conducted experiments with dual UAVs equipped for collaborative flight and vision tasks.

Swarm vs. Swarm Adaptive Search and Tracking

Designed a scalable heterogeneous UAV–UGV swarm system for autonomous detection and tracking of rogue aerial targets with adaptive coverage and search strategies.

DC Motor Neuro-Fuzzy Controller

Implemented an adaptive neuro-fuzzy control system combining RBF and MLP models for DC motor identification and control using ATmega32 microcontroller.

Online ANFIS Controller (RBF + PSO)

Designed an adaptive neuro-fuzzy inference system (ANFIS) that performs online tuning via backpropagation and RBF modeling for flexible nonlinear system control.

Inflight UAV Battery Interchange

Developed a mechanism for mid-air UAV battery replacement, enabling uninterrupted flight operations in collaboration with Monash University’s Industry Engagement Office.

Ventilator

Designed a compact ventilator system capable of providing emergency respiratory assistance with minimal user knowledge or setup requirements.

Wound Care System (NPEWT)

Developed a portable negative pressure wound therapy (NPEWT) device for treating chronic and acute wounds using controlled sub-atmospheric pressure for faster healing.

Automated External Defibrillator (AED)

Created a portable AED capable of detecting cardiac arrhythmias and administering electrical shocks automatically to prevent sudden cardiac arrest.

Drone-Delivered AEDs

Innovative system where drones rapidly deliver AED units to cardiac emergency sites, reducing response time and improving survival chances.

Raspberry Pi Remote Video

Developed a Raspberry Pi–based controller for unmanned ground vehicles with real-time wireless video streaming for enhanced situational awareness.

RF Fractional Needling Controller

Refurbished an existing fractional-needling control board: migrated from an 8-bit dsPIC design to an STM32-based controller and added new headpiece features and improved functionality.

Multi-robot Spray System

Designed a cooperative UAV–UGV spraying and mapping system using multi-objective optimization and reinforcement learning for agricultural applications in Canada.
